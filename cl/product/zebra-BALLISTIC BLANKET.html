The ballistic blanket provides flexible multipurpose protection. It can be used by de-mining units as a bomb suppression blanket, airport personnel in the fight against terrorists, cover windows and doors.

The blankets are available in sizes on request and protection levels NIJ IIA, II, and IIIA.

Standard Protection Level: V50, 17 grain fragment, 475m/sec (1560 ft/sec)
