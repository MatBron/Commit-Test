New All Around Soft Shell Appeal!
5.11 introduces it's new softshell wind and water resistant Chameleon jacket. With the huge success of our Sabre, 5.11 wanted to develop a new softshell lighter weight jacket for the public safety market, this new jacket is our Chameleon.

Both wind and water repellent and featuring a light weight mesh liner and drop tail, this jacket is perfect for any undercover, off duty assignment or just for wearing any day or night of the week.

With its three 5.11 pullout ID panels you can remain undercover or covert until the time comes when you really need to let people know who you are.

With two large hand warmer pockets with pull tabs to cinch down the waist in each and three additional interior pockets for storage the 5.11 Chameleon is sure to appeal.
