Durable and comfortable large capacity pack for caving

Information Description

Availability: In Stock
Qty:

The TRANSPORT is a particularly comfortable 45 liter backpack designed for caving. It is made of TPU (PVC-free) material, and its welded construction provides greater strength. The TRANSPORT is ideal for long-distance carries with its padded shoulder straps, waistbelt and back panel. The pack also has two molded handles.

  • Constructed of TPU (PVC-free) material
  • Welded construction for greater strength
  • Strong and abrasion resistant waterproof material
  • Shoulder straps, waistbelt and back panel are padded for greater comfort during long carries
  • Molded handles on top and side of pack
  • Ultra-strong cordlock closure
  • Upper flap with two slings and zippered pocket, key-chain and ID card pocket
