The 5.11 B-Class Uniform Shirts are the new standard and replacement for traditional uniform shirts with all of the features officers want and need.  Over a year of product development went into the design of these uniform shirts using input from police officers and police administrators.  The result is a new industry standard in B-Class uniform shirts.  Our B-Class Uniform Shirts provide a traditional appearance featuring permanent military creases to maintain a neat, clean and professional look. Performance features like our bi-swing shoulder and reinforced collar make these uniform shirts ideal for everyday wear.  B-Class Uniform Shirts are Teflon® treated for stain and dirt resistance and come with badge tabs and attached epaulets. The B-Class Uniform Shirts feature the addition of 5.11 Tactical’s patented hidden document pockets for added functionality.  The hidden document pockets of the B-Class Uniform Shirts are strong and roomy.  These pockets will not only handle your paperwork but can also be used to conceal a small handgun or any of your everyday gear such as digital recorders, cell phones or flashlights.

Our poly-rayon uniform shirts fabric is a 5.9-oz., 64% polyester/34% rayon/2% Lycra® spandex plain weave with a Teflon® finish. This produces a soft-handed fabric that is durable and wrinkle resistant. Polyester fibers hold their color for a long time and Teflon® helps prevent stains.  On the B-Class Uniform Shirts, we use only genuine melamine buttons, which are super hard and heat resistance.  These uniform shirts are triple-needle tailored and features permanent military creases.  The long sleeves feature double-layered elbows for added durability.

The B-Class Uniform Shirts with all of its features and great quality is priced competitively with the lesser quality uniform shirts on the market.  Once you wear these uniform shirts you’ll never go back to traditional uniform shirts.
