If you are headed to the range, you'll want to have our Range Ready bag at your side.  This is the ultimate in design for carrying multiple pistols to the range with all the necessary accessories and tools.  We really put our heads together to give you the ultimate in a range bag.  Our Range Ready bag was designed with input from range instructors, special operations officers, patrol officers and SWAT to give you what you need when you head out for a day on the range.  From the removable ammo tote with its brass collection bag, to the extra large side pocket with elastic magazine holders and a fold down cleaning station, this range bag can handle any type of range activity.  There is plenty of room for hearing protection, ammo, magazines, pistols, cleaning supplies and tools.  We've even included an external water bottle holder. 

Durability is the name of the game with this bag.  We've beefed this baby up to withstand the rigors of fully loaded range duty.  We use 600-denier polyester throughout to give you a long lasting bag that can handle the weight.  The carrying handles and shoulder strap are heavy duty nylon and won't fail under a heavy load.  We've added plastic feet to the bottom to keep the nylon from wearing and padded all of the pockets for added protection of your valuable firearms and accessories.  We use only quality YKK® zippers in our Range Ready bag and we've triple stitched our handles to bear the weight of the ammo, pistols and all associated gear.  We use only heavy duty D rings for our shoulder strap clips.

The quality and versatility of the Range Ready bag make this piece of equipment an exceptional value.  It is the only bag you'll need for traveling to the range with your pistols.  Leave it to 5.11 to bring quality, functionality and value to our gear bag line.
