The 5.11 B-Class Uniform Shirts are the new standard and replacement for traditional uniform shirts with all of the features officers want and need.  Over a year of product development went into the design of this uniform shirts using input from police officers and police administrators.  The result is a new industry standard in B-Class uniform shirts.  Our B-Class uniform shirts provide a traditional appearance featuring permanent military creases to maintain a neat, clean and professional look.  Performance features like diamond gusseted crotch and our Gripper self-adjusting elastic waistband make this a comfortable and durable uniform shirts.  B-Class uniform shirts are Teflon® treated for stain and dirt resistance and also come in a variety of standard uniform colors.  The B-Class uniform shirts feature the addition of 5.11 Tactical’s cargo pockets which feature a reverse elasticized pleat.  The pockets are fully compatible with our Back Up Belt System™ accessories so you can organize your gear effectively and efficiently.

Fit
Our poly-rayon uniform shirts fabric is a 7.4-oz, 65% polyester, 35% rayon twill weave with a Teflon® finish. This produces a soft-handed fabric that is durable and wrinkle resistant. Polyester fibers hold their color for a long time and Teflon® helps prevent stains.  The diamond gusseted crotch is super strong allowing you to perform even your most difficult physical duties without ripping or tearing.

Value
The B-Class uniform shirts with all of its features and great quality is priced competitively with the lesser quality uniform shirts on the market.  Once you wear these uniform shirts you’ll never go back to traditional uniform shirts.
