Durability
Designed to last with a combination of polyester, nylon, spandex and rubber, our 9” socks hold up to repeated washings. This combination of materials provides your feet with support in all the right places. The tops of the 9” socks are thinner, helping to cool your feet. The bottoms of the 9” socks at the heels and balls of your feet have extra padding for support and protection. Finally, the rubber in our 9” socks prevents them from sagging even under the most rigorous activities.
