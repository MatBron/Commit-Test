The Performance Polo Shirt is a new concept in neat, clean and professional. Made of 100% polyester, this performance polo shirt has a slight sheen and stays looking good day in and day out. The fabric dries fast to keep you comfortable and the shirt is antimicrobial which prevents the growth of odor causing bacteria.

Functionality
Made with our proprietary SmartWeave™ fabric, the Performance Polo Shirt resists snags and remains wrinkle free. This is the first snag free synthetic polo and it has all of the features of our cotton Tactical Polo. Storage
The Performance Polo Shirt has two pen pockets located on the left sleeve. This unique feature allows an operator to safely store a pen and pencil or a small pen light along the arm rather than in the button down portion of the shirt, eliminating the danger of being injured during rigorous activity

The pen pockets are stitched to endure constant use and all seams are designed for both comfort and durability. The Performance Polo Shirts makes a great duty performance polo shirt for officers requiring a casual look.
