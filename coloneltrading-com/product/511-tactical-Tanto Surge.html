The Tanto Surge is well-suited for a variety of tasks from daily use to personal defense.  The G-10 handle is comfortable in your hand and provides a sure and steady grip.  The tough PVD blade coating resists corrosion and looks great.  With its adjustable/reversible MOLLE-LOK™ attachment system, the sheath quickly attaches to web platform on your pack or tactical vest.  Remove the MOLLE-LOK™ and you can attach the sheath directly to a drop leg rig.  The Tanto Surge is a tough companion that offers numerous carry options and is great value for the price.
