Our Strobe/GPS Pouch attaches quickly to any molle compatible system keep your strobe or GPS accessible and secure.  Made of 1000D nylon with a high impact locking plastic clip this pouch is extremely durable and practical.  The GPS pouch can be easily removed and relocated to other molle compatible systems using our Slickstick™ molle attachment system.  It also snaps easily to your belt.

Slickstick™
The 5.11 Slickstick™ is compatible with standard molle attachment systems, yet functions like none other. The Slickstick™ slides through a UV-resistant ring making it easier to arrange and attach your GPS pouches and other gear. Slickstick attaches like a normal molle attachment, but it slides to make it easier. To remove an item, just pop the snap and pull the stick out.
