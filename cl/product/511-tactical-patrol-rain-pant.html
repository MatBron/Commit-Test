If you find yourself in wet weather 5.11 Tactical series has the right combination of waterproof and breathable gear for you. The perfect partner for our 3-in-1 or 5-in-1 jackets, our Patrol Rain Pants will keep you warm and dry in extreme wet conditions. Made from our high performance waterproof/breathable material, these rain pants are easy to slip on and off.

Functionality
The design of our Patrol Rain Pants lets you to easily put them on over your uniform. The elastic waistband and Prym® snap allow you to secure the rain pants just below your duty belt, giving you total access to your critical duty gear including your side-arm.

Storage
With both standard pockets and pass through pockets these rain pants give you great flexibility in wet weather conditions. You can easily access the gear in your uniform pockets via the pass through pockets or you can stow your critical gear in the standard zippered pockets of these versatile Patrol Rain Pants.

Durability
These Patrol Rain Pants are built to last. Our gusseted crotch makes them strong and provides a greater range of motion than other rain pants on the market. We’ve added abrasion resistant kick panels along the inside ankle of the pants where they are susceptible to accelerated wear.

Hardware
We use only quality YKK® zippers and Prym® snaps on our Patrol Rain Pants. Our high performance waterproof/breathable material is extremely strong and durable and can endure hours of a hard driving rain.

Fit
There are hook and loop closures at the ankles and our elastic waistband will always cling tightly to your waist protecting you from the elements.

Value
Our Patrol Rain Pants are an extreme value when you consider the quality materials and superior design. 5.11 Tactical Series prides itself on providing our customers with high quality products at a great price and these Patrol Rain Pants meet all of our mission goals.
