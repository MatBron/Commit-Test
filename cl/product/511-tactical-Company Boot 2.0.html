The 5.11 Company 2.0 station boots are a new and improved version of the traditional pull on boot that’s lightweight, supportive and cushioning.

Form
The design of the Company 2.0 boot stays true to the traditional design of the iconic pull on station boot with clean and simple lines and a classic toe down. It’s updated from the inside out with more modern materials and construction details.

Function
The key feature of the Company 2.0 is the unique 5.11 Quick Call Collar™ that uses a perforated neoprene that combines with the full grain polishable leather to provide a lightweight, supportive, cushioning upper that is second to none. On the heel is the 5.11 Kick Plate™ kick off plug to give you a natural landing target and reinforcement when taking off your boots.

The new comfort platform of the Company 2.0 boot uses our tried and true full length A.T.A.C. phylon midsole, new OrthoLite® sockliner, and composite shank that work together to provide maximum cushioning, comfort and support for any possible situation.

Fit
The Company 2.0 boots are lightweight and comfortable, ready to wear right out of the box with no break in time needed. Imported.
