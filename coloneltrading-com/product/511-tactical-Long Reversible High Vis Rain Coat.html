Keeping you Dry and Safe at the Same Time!
5.11’s new Long Reversible High Vis Rain Coat is like no other on the market today. Working closely with law enforcement officers across the country we have designed this jacket for you.
With its fully waterproof, seam sealed technology this jacket will keep you dry in extreme weather conditions.
With microphone loops and badge tab on the black side of the jacket you can count on being in uniform when you need to.
A full snap front and pass through pockets for quick access to your duty belt are a must have and on the this new 5.11 jacket.
When you reverse this jacket you will have an certified ANSI/ISEA 107-2010 Class III certified jacket using 3M Scotchlite tape and the yellow high vis materials for safety.
If that wasn’t enough the bottom of this jacket snaps off, giving you a longer chore coat for when you don’t need all of the waterproof coverage on your lower legs.
