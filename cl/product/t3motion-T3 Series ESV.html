The groundbreaking T3 Series all-electric standup vehicle (ESV) is available in an international model built to metric specifications, the T3 i-Series. This zero-gas-emission, clean-energy innovation is simple, intuitive and economical to operate. With significant breakthroughs in its proprietary power management and propulsion system, this performer delivers high "km/h" equivalent, long battery run time, short recharge time, and swappable battery modules.

It is highly stable with a low center of gravity, offers wide visibility for the driver elevated on an 22-cm platform, strikes a commanding presence, can access restricted spaces including elevators and narrow corridors, handles curbs easily, and is highly agile with a zero-degree turning radius. And it is very quiet.

The T3 i-Series is designed to enhance patrols to save driver energy and increase response times. Lockable, onboard storage is easily accessible. For maximum flexibility, two swappable battery packs mean virtually no downtime. And, with dramatically fewer parts than gasoline-powered vehicles, the T3 i-Series is high on reliability and low on maintenance. Not to mention an operating cost of around 10 cents per day.

The T3 i-Series is truly the next generation in green personal transportation solutions.
