Covert Dress Shirt Description:

  • 60% cotton/40% Poly
  • Moisture wicking
  • CovertMesh™ interior lining
  • Internal concealment chest pockets
  • RAPIDraw™ center placket to access shoulder holster
  • Long cut with curved hem
  • Hidden Button Down Collar
  • Two plaid colors in Khaki and Blue
  • Solid Blue Oxford

Plainclothes, undercover or off-duty has never been more professional and comfortable.  Our new Covert Dress Shirt offers law enforcement the ultimate in a comfortable concealment shirt that fits into any business environment.  Made from moisture wicking lightweight fabric, this shirt has a great feel and features a number of unique concealment options.  The center placket has four small metal Prym snaps hidden behind false buttons for immediate access to a shoulder holster or our own Holster Shirt.  We’ve include a CovertMesh™ lining which allows the shirt to drape over your sidearm without exposing its imprint.  The shirt is cut a bit longer with a slightly curved hem that can be worn tucked in or out.  You’ll feel secure in knowing that your side arm is not exposed to public view but is immediately accessible if you need it.  For storage versatility, there are two concealed chest pockets and a standard exterior chest pocket. You’ll be able to stow extra mags, a cell phone or a small tape recorder in our unique concealed pockets.  This shirt truly reflects the mission of 5.11 Tactical Series because it has been designed for law enforcement professionals by law enforcement professionals to provide off-duty and undercover options for concealed carry.  Make sure you pick up one in each color.
