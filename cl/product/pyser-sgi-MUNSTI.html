Description

MUNSTI is a state of the art compact, lightweight and high performance uncooled Thermal Imaging clip-on sight for use in front of any day optical sight with magnifications up to 16X and more, making it ideally suitable for sniper weapons, anti-materiel weapons and any weapon where the user wishes to leave the daysight in place.

It allows detection ranges up to 2760m (man) and 7000m (vehicle), allows the firer to attach it instantly by its over-centre throw lever shock absorber base on to the Picatinny rail, and switch on to use within eight seconds without affecting the zero of the day sight.

MUNSTI is available with thermal detectors of either 388x284 or 640x480 resolutions and with a choice of optics giving either 7 degrees ( 125 mils) or 12 degrees
(210 mils) horizontal fields of view.

Affordable and virtually maintenance free, its fully featured simple and intuitive push button controls make it the ideal choice for Special Forces, Police elite units and regular forces requiring the best in night vision sights.

MUNSTI is designed, developed and manufactured in the UK by Pyser-SGI and requires only a UK export licence.
