The LMC™ Modified Clip Point is a handy handful utilitarian knife. It’s a mid-size folder that cuts like a big knife and is comfortable in your hand. The full-size feel and total control comes from the finger coil at the base of the blade and a full size grip. An excellent choice for everyday cutting jobs, the 5.11 LMC Modified Clip Point looks as sharp as it cuts. The satin finish plain edge is perfect for routine and serious cutting jobs and the V-hole in the blade provides instant one-hand operation. Textured FRN scales are skeletonized for optimal grip with bare or gloved hands, wet or dry, and the jumping at the back of the spine ensures complete control. Two pocket clip locations accommodate right and left hand users.
