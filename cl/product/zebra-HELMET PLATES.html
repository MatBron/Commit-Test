Zebra Armours Add-on Rifle Protection stops several different types of high velocity rounds in the crucial front and rear areas of the helmet. Recommended when exposed to AK47 threat, (7.62x39mm) @ 720m/sec).
     

Our ceramic / composite backing laminated plates come in multi-curve or single-curve shapes. Sizes are 200x250mm (8”x10”) and 250x300mm (10”x12”). We manufacture ceramic with Aramid, Polyethylene, and Fibreglass backing. The plates can be standalone plates (SAP) or combination plates (CP) to be used with minimum level II body armour. Anti-Spall System can be added to the plates to insure that no ceramic or bullet fragments exit the plate. This adds 200g to the 250x300mm plate.
