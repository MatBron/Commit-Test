Best Duty Style Jacket on the Market!

It doesn’t get much better than the new 5.11 Signature Duty Jacket. The 5.11 development team working closely with law enforcement officers has designed a jacket that we are proud to call our Signature style.

Its fully waterproof, breathable seam sealed technology will allow you to say completely warm, dry and comfortable in even the most extreme weather conditions. The waterproof technology is also blood borne pathogen resistant, helping to keep you safe.

With its heavier zip out liner, this jacket can keep you warm in just about any environment.

The removable or stowable waterproof hood, zip through collar, and large 5.11 rear pullout ID panel gives you the function and features you need.

In additional to all of this we have added the 5.11 Document pockets on the front chest for storage of additional paperwork or any other items.

The two bellowed duty pockets on the front of the jacket incorporate the 5.11 BBS system with two separate hand warmer pockets.

Inside the jacket you will find an additional storage pocket large enough for your note pad.

The bi-swing back and dual side zippers allow for quick access to your duty belt and side arm.
