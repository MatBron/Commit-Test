Our new turnout bags are available in three mission-specific sizes. The Squad 2400 Personal Bag is built to carry your personal clothing items. The dimensions of the main compartment are: 20” x 11” x 11” giving you 2420 cubic inches of storage space. The two exterior pockets each measure 8.5” x 8.5” x 3” giving you an additional 866 cubic inches of storage. The Squad 2400 Personal Bag has a waterproof bottom to protect your valuable gear as well as heavy duty grab and haul handles for all that weight.

Featuring leather accents for style and a light color interior to make it easy to find your gear, this bag will hold up the extremes of day to day use. The Squad 2400 Personal Bag is made from 1050 denier DWR double-coated (durable water-resistant) nylon. The Squad 2400 Personal Bags have the 5.11 LockJaw™ opening that makes it easy to stow and access your critical gear. A polyester liner bag is included that can be removed and washed when it gets dirty.

This Squad 2400 Personal Bag is great for embroidery and we’ve included our exclusive WriteBar™ for labeling. It also features a flag holder and ID window. Finally, we’ve included a non-slip padded shoulder strap for those long hauls.

You can always count on 5.11 Tactical Series to bring you the innovation of modern construction materials, combine them for functional use and adjust to the traditional needs of Fire fighting and EMS.
