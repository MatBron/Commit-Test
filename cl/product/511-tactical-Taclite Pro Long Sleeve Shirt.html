Taclite™ Pro Long Sleeve Shirt Description

  • Durable, ultra-lightweight 4-oz., 65% polyester/35% cotton ripstop

  • 5.11’s patented hidden document pockets

The Taclite™ Pro Long Sleeve Shirt is the perfect complement to the 5.11 Taclite™ Pro Pants. The 5.11 Taclite™ Pro Long Sleeve Shirt is a comfortable, durable and functional choice for law enforcement, military and fire professionals. Ideal for both on and off-duty wear, the innovative 5.11 Taclite™ Pro Long Sleeve Shirts have 5.11’s exceptional fit, patented hidden document pockets and unmatched heat management system.

Functionality
The Taclite™ Pro Long Sleeve Shirts have hidden button-down collar stays and a handsome, functional design offering a professional appearance that is also appropriate for casual environments. With versatility like this, you can swiftly transition between work, play and travel without changing clothes. 

Storage
Stash airline tickets, maps, your cell phone or a small backup in 5.11’s patented hidden document pockets located on the front chest of the 5.11 Taclite™ Pro Long Sleeve Shirt. Discreetly designed with a Velcro® closure and one-handed access, the strong, roomy pockets work in tandem with 5.11® Undergear Holster Shirts. Overlaying the hidden document pockets are large, pleated patch pockets finished with Velcro®. The left pocket incorporates a dual pass-through slot for pens, chem-lights and tire gauges. Two narrow pen pockets are placed on the left sleeve, for six pockets in all.

Durability
Made of ultra-lightweight, 4-oz., 65% polyester/35% cotton ripstop, the 5.11 Taclite™ Pro Long Sleeve Shirts lets you work, play and travel in comfort and style. Triple needle construction and reinforced anti-crack, anti-chip melamine buttons result in an amazingly hard-wearing shirt.

Hardware
In addition to its breathable fabrics and strategic construction, the 5.11 Taclite™ Pro Long Sleeve Shirts employ a large cape-back with a tacked center point covers moisture-wicking Drilex® material to efficiently vent and evaporate moisture around the clock. Stitched eyelets at each gusseted underarm further dispel heat and moisture to keep you cool.

Fit
There’s a high-performance 5.11 Taclite™ Pro Long Sleeve Shirt for every mission and activity. With several colors from which to choose, you can wear the shirt that best fits your daily plans and climate. Both short and long sleeved versions are available.

Value
