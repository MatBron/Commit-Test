Description

Bicam and Tricam changeover cameras are a series of 24/7 surveillance cameras employing a unique changeover technology with 2 or 3 sensors chosen to optimise picture quality dependent on conditions.

Each system incorporates pan and tilt tripod, power source control and 6.4" TFT rugged waterproof monitor. 

Any combination of high quality colour, high sensitivity monochrome, or image intensified camera can be married to the lens chosen and housed in an environmental housing ready for installation to a new or existing CCTV system.

Zoom Lens
