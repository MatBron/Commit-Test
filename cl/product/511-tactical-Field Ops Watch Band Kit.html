The Field Ops Watch Band Kit keeps your watch looking new. The replacement band is attached by a pin and is easy to install. As usual, 5.11 Tactical Series is always trying to meet your needs in the tactical arena. These watch bands are a great for keeping your Field Ops Watch looking great. The watch band comes in two different sections. This item is the buckle section of the field ops watchband. Imported.

The Kit includes the following parts:

  • 1 Band with no buckle
  • 1 Regular length band with buckle
  • 1 Long length band with buckle
  • 1 Pin assembly
  • 1 Pin wrench

Fits original and current versions of the Field Ops watch.
The band is attached to the watch case with a pin and is easy to install.
