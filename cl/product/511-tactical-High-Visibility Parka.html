If you work in a hazardous environment requiring high visibility 24 hours a day, you need this jacket.  Our Hi-Visibility Parka gives you the protection you need under any and all conditions.  This is a bright yellow ANSI III rated Hi-Visibility Parka featuring 3M Scotchlite™ reflective tape.  If you are working traffic at night you’ll stand out to on-coming vehicles even in the rain.  The Hi-Visibility Parka features our high performance waterproof/breathable seam-sealed construction so you’ll stay dry and warm.  We’ve included microphone loops and a array of pockets. The hidden chest pockets are Back-Up-Belt System™ compatible for concealing a small handgun or stowing critical gear using our compatible pouches.  We’ve also included a removable hood that can be stored in the collar or rear pocket.

We use quality YKK zippers and Prym Snaps throughout so you can depend on the quality of this great piece of outerwear.  There are side zippers for ventilation and access to your duty belt along with snaps for keeping out the cold and rain when needed.

Once again, 5.11 Tactical Series has gone the extra mile in innovation and quality to give service professionals the tools they need to perform their jobs and stay safe.  You can depend on the Hi-Visibility Parka to perform when you need visibility and protection from the elements.
