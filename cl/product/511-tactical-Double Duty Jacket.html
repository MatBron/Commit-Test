Double Duty!

Our new Double Duty Jacket will work two ways, with or without the lightweight zip out liner. The wind and water repellent outer shell will keep you dry and the liner will keep you warm. The above the belt, ike length styling is designed for use on duty and works great with your duty belt.

In additional to all of this we have added the 5.11 Document pockets on the front chest for storage of additional paperwork or any other items.

With its bi-swing back for extra range of motion, its zip through collar and elasticized cuff and hem this jacket was designed by end users for end users.

Incorporating two bellowed duty pockets with the 5.11 BBS system in each and two hand warmer pockets behind them for extra storage.

The jacket also has the underarm zippers for added breathability or for embroidery or screen printing access.
