With the same durability, functionality and innovation as our best-selling 5.11 Tactical Pant, the 5.11 Tactical Short is also preferred by law enforcement, military and fire professionals for both on and off-duty wear. The women’s fit cotton 5.11 Tactical Short comes in 3 colors, with size ranges to fit almost everyone. Like the 5.11 Tactical Pant, the sturdy, seven-pocket 5.11 Tactical Short is complete with 5.11’s trademark rear slash pockets and utility strap.

Because the setup of 5.11 Tactical Short is very similar to the 5.11 Tactical Pant, your gear can be organized exactly the same way. So when you draw your firearm, it will be right where you expect it to be.

Made of tough, 8.5-oz. 100% cotton canvas that softens with every wash, the 5.11 Tactical Short takes on the demands of work, play and travel. Known for superior comfort, functionality and fit, the shorts feature a 8” inseam and a self-adjusting comfort waistband that helps you bend and stretch with ease. Double and triple-needle construction is utilized throughout, plus 48 bartacks in high-stress areas.

Front pockets are strong and roomy; inverted pleats on the cargo pockets remain flat until needed for a neat, professional appearance. A narrow front pocket accommodates a magazine, cell phone or utility knife. The extra deep rear slash pockets are easily accessed and hold extended mags, a baton or a 15” flashlight. You can also use the utility strap to secure a flashlight or store your protective ear gear at the range.

The 5.11 Tactical Short utilizes a proprietary weave that binds in on itself to prevent raveling. For added durability, the magazine/cell phone pocket and corners of the slash pockets are lined with Cordura™ nylon. The double reinforced seat provides enhanced abrasion protection.

On the 5.11 Tactical Short, we use only genuine, locking-flange YKK™ zippers and quality PRYM™ snaps, both of which are top-of-the-line in the industry. Additional quality hardware is found in the front, where a convenient D-ring holds your keys, ID or duty knife.

As with the 5.11 Tactical Pant, the legendary fit of the women’s cut 5.11 Tactical Short is a closely-guarded secret based on a tried-and-true formula created 30 years ago. Designed for active movement in changing conditions, the shorts perform and look great on women of all shapes and sizes. For best results, pair the 5.11 Tactical Short with the 5.11 Professional Polo or 5.11 Tactical Shirt.
