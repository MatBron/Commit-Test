Description

Vertigo is a unique solution to high powered day and night long distance surveillance where a conventional high performance camera would be seen by the targets.  The unique chimney form disguises perfectly the sensor system and allows full pan/tilt and zoom functions in an extremely covert package.

Specifications

  • IP66 rated
  • 12 V DC via 26 pin connector, male
  • Pan speed up to 12°/sec
  • Tilt speed up to 6°/sec
  • Pan angle 350° (minimum)
  • Tilt angle -30° - + 10°


Control Box
