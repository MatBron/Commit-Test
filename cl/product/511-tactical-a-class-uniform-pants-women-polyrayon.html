The 5.11 A-Class Pant is the new standard and replacement for traditional uniform pants with all of the features officers want and need. Over a year of product development went into the design of these uniform pants using input from police officers and police administrators. The result is a new industry standard in A-Class uniform pants. Our A-Class uniform pants provide a traditional appearance featuring permanent military creases to maintain a neat, clean and professional look. Performance features like diamond gusseted crotch and Our Gripper waistband make these uniform pants comfortable and durable. A-Class uniform pants are Teflon® treated for stain and dirt resistance and also come in a variety of standard uniform colors.

The pockets of the A Class uniform pants have been designed for easy access and storage. Even with your pockets loaded, the uniform pants maintain professional lines.
