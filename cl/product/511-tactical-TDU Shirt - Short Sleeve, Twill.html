The 5.11 Tactical Duty Uniform Twill Shirt is the new standard and replacement of the old style BDU. Designed with today’s law enforcement or security force in mind this shirt has all the features officers want and need. Over a year of product development went into the design of this shirt using input from police officers, special operations operators, and military personnel. The result is a new industry standard in BDU style shirts. Made of tough, 5.78 oz. cotton twill, the 5.11 TDU® Twill Shirt stands up to the rigors of patrol and special operations. Treated for stain and soil resistant, this shirt stays looking neat clean and professional for the entire duty shift. For best results, pair the 5.11 TDU® Twill Shirt with the 5.11 TDU® Twill Pant.

The chest pockets of the 5.11 Tactical Duty Uniform Twill Shirt are strong and roomy. Below the chest pockets, you’ll find our patented hidden document pockets. These pockets will not only handle your paperwork but can also be used to conceal a small handgun or any of your everyday gear such as digital recorders, cell phones or flashlights.

The 5.11 TDU® Twill Shirt utilizes 65%polyester/35% cotton twill material that resist tearing and abrasion. Triple stitched and bartacked in all stress areas, this shirt will hold up even under the most difficult conditions. We use only genuine crack and heat resistant melamine buttons and added extra thread to hold them in place under the stress of hard work. This shirt is extremely tough.

With comfort being the name of the game in today’s uniform design this shirt has all the comfort features an operator would want. The bi-swing shoulder offers superior range of motion and flexibility. The 5.11 TDU® Twill Shirt with all of its new features and great quality is priced competitively with the lesser quality BDU’s on the market. Once you wear this shirt you’ll never go back to a traditional BDU.
