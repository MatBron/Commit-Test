Looking for a multi-functional, medium sized gear bag that can conceal a firearm, hold a laptop and accommodate all of your day-to-day gear? Well, look no farther. 5.11 Tactical’s Side Trip Bag serves as a briefcase, gear bag and travel bag all in one. We’ve packed this businesslike bag with features that will offer the operator or officer a professional looking and durable briefcase. A large hidden pocket with our Backup Belt System™/Velcro®-compatible panel provides you with a compartment to secure a firearm and associated accessories. The pocket entrance is just under the carry handle and sealed with Velcro® for quick access. The inner lining is lined with loop Velcro® which allows you to attach our holster pouch to accommodate a handgun. The interior of the bag is a light grey for easy viewing of your stowed gear and the large exterior padded pocket can handle a laptop computer. The main compartment has a MOLLE/ALICE compatible panel for attaching additional MOLLE compatible pouches. The two smaller outer pockets feature organizational panels to help you keep your gear organized. Also the two side pockets are expandable for carrying water bottles or larger items. Like all 5.11 products, our Side Trip Bag has been tested and reviewed by officers and operators. We’ve taken their input to make sure this is not your ordinary nylon briefcase.

Durability
Durability is the name of the game with our Side Trip Bag. We use 1000-denier nylon throughout to give you a long lasting bag that can handle the weight of all your gear and accessories. The carrying handles are heavy-duty nylon with molded plastic lockable grips and won't fail under a heavy load. We use only quality YKK® zippers in our Side Trip Bag. We’ve triple stitched our handles to bear the weight of the your gear. This bag is designed to last so you’ll be carrying it for years to come.
