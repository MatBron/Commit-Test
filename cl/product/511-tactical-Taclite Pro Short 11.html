Based on the success of our popular Taclite® Tactical Short, 511 introduces the 11” inch inseam version. We’ve talked to first responders around the globe who are seeking a longer cut for functional and style purposes. The short incorporates all the great functionality you have come to expect from the leading pant and short brand in the industry. The short features a lightweight, breathable, poly-cotton ripstop fabric with a Teflon® coating and a fully gusseted crotch that allows for a full range of movement. Two cargo pockets are located on both thighs and a magazine / cell phone pocket is located over the left cargo pocket for quick access with your off hand. An external knife pocket on the right front side gives you easy access to a 5.11® knife. The magazine and knife pocket are reinforced with 100% Cordura® nylon. We have kept our signature slash pockets in the rear along with the strap on our 5.11 Tactical® Pants. The belt loops are thick enough to support a large belt to carry the weight of as sidearm, cuffs or other equipment. We use only YKK® zippers, Prym® snaps and Horn® buttons to maximize durability.
